The Revolutionary 1970s

The decade that gave birth to the "Angry Young Man" and revolutionary music

Iconic Movies

Sholay (1975)

The greatest curry Western that embodied everything Bollywood stands for. A film that redefined Indian cinema with its epic storytelling and memorable characters.

Deewaar (1975)

The film that cemented Amitabh Bachchan's 'Angry Young Man' image. A crime drama that reflected the socio-economic realities of 1970s India.

Revolutionary Music

R.D. Burman Revolution

The musical genius who brought Western instruments and innovative sounds to Indian cinema. His compositions in the 70s revolutionized Bollywood music.

Yaadon Ki Baaraat (1973)

The soundtrack that defined the masala film genre with memorable tracks. R.D. Burman's compositions became the template for Bollywood music.

The Glamorous 1980s

The disco era that brought glamour, special effects, and electronic beats

Blockbuster Movies

Mr. India (1987)

A groundbreaking sci-fi superhero film that introduced special effects to Indian cinema. Featured the iconic villain Mogambo and memorable music.

Qayamat Se Qayamat Tak (1988)

The film that launched Aamir Khan and revived the romantic genre in Bollywood. Its music became synonymous with young love in Indian cinema.

Disco Era Music

Bappi Lahiri - The Disco King

The flamboyant composer who brought disco and electronic music to Bollywood. His golden chains and synthesized beats defined 80s Indian music.

Usha Uthup - The Pop Queen

The versatile singer who dominated Hindi pop music through the 80s and 90s. Her baritone voice and traditional attire made her an icon of Indian pop culture.

The Romantic 1990s

The golden age of romance, family values, and melodious music

Romantic Classics

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)

The biggest romantic hit in Indian cinema history. DDLJ defined modern Bollywood romance and became a cultural phenomenon that still resonates today.

Hum Aapke Hain Koun..! (1994)

A celebration of Indian family values and traditions. This musical family drama redefined the concept of clean entertainment in Bollywood.

Melodious Voices

Kumar Sanu - The King of Romance

The voice that defined 90s Bollywood romance with his melodious renditions. His songs became the soundtrack of an entire generation's love stories.

The Golden Trio: Kumar Sanu, Udit Narayan & Alka Yagnik

The legendary singers whose voices defined the romantic era of 90s Bollywood. Their collaborations created the most memorable love songs in Indian cinema.
